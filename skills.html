HARDSIDE
Main Problem
The main disadvantage of working at gamedev is professional burnout. As a result, after a long period of work in game development, you may encounter aversion to games or lose interest in them for a short period of time. The most important thing before committing yourself to the video game industry is to understand why you came there, what you want to achieve and what you are striving for. Do you have the required "knowledge base" for a particular specialty, it does not mean that you will succeed. You will have to study even after mastering the basic skills of the chosen specialty, so only a persistent and flexible candidate can handle it.

Hobby

don't make it your main source of income. making games is a fun hobby that, under certain conditions, can make good money. but as soon as game development becomes work, routine and disgust appear.

Great dynamism

The market changes very quickly, so it is almost impossible to plan an entire project from start to finish. It's hard to work in this industry for people who like stability, predictability and orderliness.

Creative

There are times when all the creative ends, and we all go from top to bottom on the bug tracker, without looking back at the "specialization" of the programmer.
